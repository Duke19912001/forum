el3issa wrote:
a noob question from a noob player

what am i supposed to do to win a game


i have never played total war game but i though i migh learn this game it seems good game but i dont know what im doing wrong


here is a video of my gameplay


https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yDi3BO1Vauo&feature=c4-overview&list=UUFzEykziawuPRUR0s81zsXQ


any advices !!!


1) At 9:00 when you attack the siege: Listen to the advisor, he said, put your archers up the hill. Then shot from there at only one enemy army (the nearest to your achers), then they will run up the hill to your archers...you have an advantage over them because of that. Now at a certain point they gonna retreat, in the meantime, let your melee army attack the siege weapons and move up with your archers so you attack the other enemy melee troops. Hint: lure the enemy with archers to attack you.
2) At 13:00 you dont need to attack the siege anymore 
3) At 14:00 put only 1 or 2 from your troops to avoid that they fall in your back when you go the right path. 1 archer 1 melee is enough. Then move with the others to the path and put ur troops in a good line of defensive.

Other hints, group one of the same type army with strg+lmb to highlight them and press strg+1 (2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9,0), then you dont need to click every troops. And also while moving with right click, hold that done and move your mouse. Its very important to keep your troops in a line to the enemy so they cant flank you. So for you its the same, try to fall into enemys flanks to gain an advantage. Thats also what frorrests are foor, you see the symbol when they are hidden. And further in game, dont attack with your whole army/troops, always keep a reserve for suprises either from your or the enemy.
