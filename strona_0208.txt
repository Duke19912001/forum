You don't really need to hex edit anything to play with mods. Just edit your user.script.txt file in appdata/roaming/the creative assembly/rome2/scripts. Mine looks like this:

mod "_divide_et_impera_release_1.pack";
mod "_divide_et_impera_release_2.pack";
mod "@@@@@@11dfixpack.pack"

Everything inside the quotation marks are the names of the modfiles in the data folder.
