Quote:
*by Igec35*
There is a solution for steam mods. Just follow these steps:

1. First you use downloader(http://steamworkshopdownloader.com).
2. Download wanted mod from steam workshop and name it ->name of the mod(whatever you want)<-.pack.File must have .pack extension.
3. Open mod with hexa editor and you'll find bytes PFH4. Delete all first bytes untill PFH4. It is important to make sure PFH4 is the very start of your new moded file.
4. Save it and put it to your RTW2 data folder.
5. Use user.script to activate the mod and play it.

Cheers.
