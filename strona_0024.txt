1. you need the reloaded game installed WITH their crack for this to work
2. you need 10 gb of free space for the installer to unpack the data files, modify them and pack them back. Also worth mentioning, the installer does this in the windows temp folder, so if you have a small windows partition with less than 10 gb of free space the update will fail
